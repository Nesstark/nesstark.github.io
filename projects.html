1st semester project

A Night Out: Designing a Transformational Video Game to Promote Awareness Towards LGBTQ+ Violence

This research paper explores the design of a transformational video game aimed at raising awareness about the violence faced by LGBTQ+ communities. Through an engaging and impactful narrative, the game seeks to foster empathy and understanding by immersing players in experiences reflecting real-world challenges. This innovative project demonstrates the potential of video games as tools for social change and education.

School project part of IP course

OpenCVGame

This project was a part of the Image Processing course at Aalborg University. We students where tasked with making an opencv python game. It could be anything we wanted. I chose to create this small game, where you would control a rocket with your face, with face tracking from opencv. You would then travel the correct distance to the exosphere with the speed of one, spaceX's booster rockets.
